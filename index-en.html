With more than 20 professors from all TH Koln faculties, the THK-AI Research Cluster is one of the largest AI research collaborations at a German university of applied sciences.

Why THK-AI?

Applied AI at scale

THK-AI combines modern compute infrastructure and interdisciplinary expertise to accelerate applied AI from prototype to deployment.

Open for collaboration

The cluster supports cooperative projects between associations, companies, professors, and students across disciplines.

Strong infrastructure

A distributed high-performance setup in Gummersbach and Leverkusen enables resilient research workflows and 24/7 web access.

THK-AI Example Project

The project THK-KIplus (TH Koln - Kunstliche Intelligenz plus) was funded from June 2023 to November 2025 in the federal program KI-Nachwuchs@FH by the Federal Ministry for Research, Technology and Space.

The initiative received funding of around EUR 1.3 million and established one of the strongest AI-oriented research infrastructures at German universities of applied sciences.

CAIRNE Gold Member

THK-AI is a Gold Member of the CAIRNE Research Network (Confederation of Laboratories for Artificial Intelligence Research in Europe).

CAIRNE is a European non-profit AI community with a human-centred focus. It connects research institutions, industry, and policy stakeholders to strengthen AI excellence, collaboration, and digital sovereignty across Europe.

# ShapeFuture YouTube channel now online

![Banner of the official ShapeFuture YouTube channel](../../figures/news/shape-future/shapefuture-youtube-teaser.jpg){fig-alt="Banner of the ShapeFuture YouTube channel"}

`2026-04-17`

The EU-funded ShapeFuture project has launched its official YouTube channel. The channel publishes project results, demonstrations, interviews, and outreach material. It is available at [youtube.com/@ShapeFutureProject](https://www.youtube.com/@ShapeFutureProject).

Responsible for the work at TH Köln is [Prof. Dr. Thomas Bartz-Beielstein](https://www.th-koeln.de/personen/thomas.bartz-beielstein/), together with PhD students Noah C. Pütz and Jens Uwe Brandt. The team contributes research on robust and interpretable AI methods to ShapeFuture.

date: 2026-01-12

Prof. Dr. Thomas Bartz-Beielstein feels honored to accept the invitation
to continue serving as an Associate Editor for the **[ACM Transactions
on Evolutionary Learning and Optimization
(TELO)](https://dl.acm.org/journal/telo/editorial-board)** in 2026.

![](../figures/news/acm_telo_2026-1.jpg)
https://dl.acm.org/journal/telo/editorial-board

ACM TELO is a high-quality journal dedicated to bridging the gap between
machine learning and optimization. It publishes original research on
evolutionary computation, Bayesian optimization, and related fields. A
unique feature of TELO is its review model, where Associate Editors
first write a review invited by an Area Editor before taking over the
editorial role, ensuring both high-quality feedback and well-informed
decisions. Prof. Bartz-Beielstein is looking to another year of
contributing to this exciting field and supporting the community!\

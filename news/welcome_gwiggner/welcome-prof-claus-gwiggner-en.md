# Welcome Prof. Dr. Claus Peter Gwiggner to the THK-AI Research Cluster

The THK-AI Research Cluster is delighted to welcome [Prof. Dr. Claus Peter Gwiggner](https://www.th-koeln.de/personen/claus.gwiggner/) as its newest member. With his addition, the cluster grows to 26 members from across the faculties of TH Köln and gains depth in operations research, decision-making under uncertainty, and causal statistical inference.

## Short biography

Prof. Dr. Claus Peter Gwiggner teaches and conducts research at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering Sciences of TH Köln. He is affiliated with the *Institute for Data Science, Engineering, and Analytics (IDE+A)* on Campus Gummersbach.

His research focuses on supply chain analytics and stochastic optimisation. In teaching he covers discrete optimisation, causal statistical inference, decision-making under uncertainty, and *Decision Intelligence / Operations Research*. He is a member of the German Operations Research Society (GOR) and a recipient of fellowships from DAAD, EGIDE, and the Franco-German University. In 2024, one of his projects received the Wirtschaftswoche *Best of Consulting* award in the *Purchase & Supply Chain Management* category.

## Connection to AI research

Gwiggner's work sits at an interface the THK-AI Research Cluster has so far covered only partially: data-driven optimisation and causal inference are the methodological backbone of many modern AI applications in which decisions must be taken under incomplete information — from supply chain and logistics models to predictive maintenance and robust recommendation systems. His expertise in decision intelligence complements the cluster's existing strengths in machine learning and optimisation with a markedly decision-oriented perspective.

We extend a warm welcome to Prof. Gwiggner and look forward to working together!

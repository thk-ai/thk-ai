# ECiP @ GECCO 2026: Bartz-Beielstein organizes the industry track in Costa Rica

![GECCO 2026 in San Antonio de Belén, Costa Rica](../../figures/news/ecip-gecco-2026/gecco-hero.png){fig-alt="GECCO 2026 banner, Costa Rica"}

`2026-06-18`

[Prof. Dr. Thomas Bartz-Beielstein](https://www.th-koeln.de/personen/thomas.bartz-beielstein/) is once again organizing the *Evolutionary Computation in Practice* (ECiP) track at this year's Genetic and Evolutionary Computation Conference (GECCO 2026). GECCO takes place from 13 to 17 July 2026 in San Antonio de Belén near Alajuela, Costa Rica; the ECiP session is scheduled for Thursday, 16 July 2026, 17:00–18:00.

Since 2002 — for more than two decades — ECiP has connected academic research with industrial practice. Renowned speakers from academia and industry share how evolutionary optimization is applied in real-world projects beyond the textbook. The track is held in high regard within the community and is a fixture of the GECCO program.

Two speakers have already confirmed for 2026: Nicolás Álvarez Gil (ArcelorMittal Global R&D, Spain) and Ryoki Hamano (CyberAgent, Inc., Japan). Further program details will appear soon on the [ECiP website](https://sequential-parameter-optimization.github.io/evolutionary-computation-in-practice/).
